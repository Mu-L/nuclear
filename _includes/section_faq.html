Frequently Asked Questions

Is Nuclear Music Player legal?

Nuclear is a music player that aggregates content from free, legal sources on the internet. It functions similarly to a web browser, allowing you to access publicly available content.

How does Nuclear protect my privacy?

We collect absolutely no data and have no telemetry. Nuclear is completely private and respects your freedom. Your listening habits are yours alone.

What makes Nuclear different from other music players?

Nuclear is free (as in freedom), open-source, and has no ads or tracking. It lets you stream from multiple sources in one place, with a modern interface and plugin system.

Does Nuclear have a music library?

No, Nuclear does not have servers and we don't host any music. With Nuclear, you either bring your own music, or listen to what can be found on the internet
